**Established:** 23 February 2026
**Authority:** Nigel (Project Owner / Civil Engineer)
**Principle:** Pareto (80/20)

---

## THE RULE

**Minimum 80% compliance score at each review level before advancing to the next.**

| Gate | From | To | Minimum Score | Basis |
|------|------|----|---------------|-------|
| Gate 1 | Level 1 (Speed Review) | Level 2 (Deep Dive) | **80/100** | Pareto — 80% of issues resolved on first pass |
| Gate 2 | Level 2 (Deep Dive) | Level 3 (Precision Audit) | **80/100** | Pareto — 80% of deeper issues resolved |
| Gate 3 | Level 3 (Precision Audit) | Publication / Sign-Off | **80/100** | Pareto — 80% of granular issues resolved |

---

## LEVEL DEFINITIONS

| Level | Metaphor | Focus | Depth |
|-------|----------|-------|-------|
| **Level 1** | The Tally (clay tokens) | Big shapes — navigation, structure, findability, major errors | Broad sweep |
| **Level 2** | The Ledger (double-entry) | Cross-referencing, translation accuracy, framework rebuild, consistency | Detailed verification |
| **Level 3** | The Algorithm (computation) | Every number, every image, every cross-reference, every translation key | Granular precision |

## ACCEPTED EXCEPTIONS

Items formally documented as "known beta/PoC limitation" may be excluded from scoring IF:
1. They are explicitly acknowledged on the site itself (not hidden)
2. They are documented in the review brief so reviewers know not to penalise
3. The project owner has signed off on each exception

Current accepted exceptions:
- **Translation coverage** — images are English-only, known PoC constraint
- **Game login gate** — game requires authentication by design for beta testing

## THE METAPHOR

The review levels mirror the dual evolution of numbers and words shown in the 12 Knowledge Web images on the landing page:
- Numbers came first (accounting before writing — Schmandt-Besserat)
- Words caught up, then merged with numbers, then became code
- Each level encodes more detail, like humanity's recording systems evolved from pictograms to Unicode

The 80% gate ensures each encoding layer is solid before the next is built on top.
