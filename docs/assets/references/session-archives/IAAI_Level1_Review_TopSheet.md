## WHY THIS MATTERS — READ THIS FIRST

This is your coursework. It will be graded, ranked, and published.

The Infrastructure Academy teaches that civilisation's two oldest technologies — **numbers** and **words** — evolved together on the same artefacts. Sumerian clay tokens counted grain before cuneiform told stories. Accounting preceded writing. Numbers before narrative. You can see this dual evolution in the **12 Knowledge Web images** on the landing page of the site you are about to review.

This review follows the same pattern:

- **Level 1** (this exercise): The tally. Count the errors, find the big shapes. Speed review.
- **Level 2** (next): The ledger. Cross-reference, verify, connect the threads. Deep dive.
- **Level 3** (final): The algorithm. Every number, every image, every cross-reference, every translation key. Precision audit.

You are Level 1. Your job is to be fast, accurate, and honest. The 20 of you will be scored against each other. The best performers advance to Level 2. This is how the Academy works — the same way civilisation refined its tools from tallies to calculus.

**Your first task:** Find the platform's **Mission** and **Vision** statements on the website. State them in your own words. If you cannot find them, or if what you find is unclear, that is a critical site failure — and you earn bonus XP for identifying it.
