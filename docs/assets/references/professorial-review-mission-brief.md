## REVIEW CRITERIA

Score each area on a scale of 1–5 (1 = Critical Failure, 5 = Excellent):

### A. ARCHITECTURAL COHERENCE (Weight: 20%)
- Can a first-time visitor understand the 9 Warp & Weft threads?
- Is the relationship between the 14 Relays, 5 Webs, and 4 Web Types clear?
- Can you navigate from any page to any framework element within 3 clicks?
- Is the site architecture consistent with the educational mission?
- Does the cataloguing system meet university library standards?

### B. CONTENT ACCURACY (Weight: 25%)
- Are relay names, numbers, and chapter titles consistent across ALL pages, ALL volumes, and ALL game data?
- Are the 4Cs correctly stated everywhere (Conflagration/War, Consumption/Famine, Conflict/Disease, Climate/Existential)?
- Are framework definitions consistent between pages?
- Are dates, facts, and historical claims accurate and sourced?
- Do images match their context and load correctly?

### C. CONTENT COMPLETENESS (Weight: 20%)
- Are all 14 relays (R0–R13) covered in all 3 volumes?
- Are the 4 Web Types defined and visible on the site (not buried in JSON)?
- Is the Warp & Weft framework explained anywhere a visitor can find it?
- Are all 17 framework layers (A–Q from MASTER_INDEX) accessible?
- Are all tables, charts, and data visualisations present where expected?

### D. CROSS-REFERENCING & INDEXING (Weight: 15%)
- Can you search for any relay, web, web type, scholar, or framework element and find it?
- Are the 11 index/catalogue files consistent with each other?
- Does every image have a known location, subject tag, and relay mapping?
- Are campaign documents cross-referenced to the site content they describe?
- Would a university librarian approve this cataloguing system?

### E. TRANSLATION QUALITY (Weight: 10%)
- Does the language switcher work for all 8 languages?
- Are translations complete (no English fallback text showing in non-English modes)?
- Are framework terms translated consistently?
- Do RTL languages (Arabic) display correctly?

### F. USER EXPERIENCE & NAVIGATION (Weight: 10%)
- Are there dead ends (pages with no way back)?
- Is the mobile experience functional?
- Is the progression from Story → Guide → Game clear?
- Does DAVID (the AI DM) effectively guide users?
