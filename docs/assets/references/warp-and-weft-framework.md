## The Metaphor

The Weave is named after **Relay 7: The Loom — The Binary Birth**. The mechanical loom was the first machine to systematically interlace warp and weft threads at industrial scale, standardising the continental rhythm and seeding the Industrial Revolution. In the same way, this framework interlaces the **5 Great Webs** (warp) with the **4 Web Types** (weft) to reveal the pattern of civilisation across 12,000 years and 12 Relays.

5 Warp + 4 Weft = **9 threads** woven through all 12 Civilizational Relays.

## THE 4 WEFT: Web Types

The weft threads classify the *nature* of infrastructure at each stage. They map directly onto the 12 Relays in sequence, representing the evolutionary arc from Earth's own systems to artificial intelligence.

| # | Web Type | Character | Relay Range | Colour |
|---|----------|-----------|-------------|--------|
| 1 | **Natural** | Earth's own systems — fire, trees, rivers, horses | Relays 1–4 (Fire → Horse) | #22c55e |
| 2 | **Machine** | Engineered physical systems — roads, ships, rail, looms | Relays 5–8 (Roads → Loom) | #3b82f6 |
| 3 | **Information** | Data and signal systems — engine, AAA triad, orbit | Relays 9–11 (Engine → Orbit) | #f59e0b |
| 4 | **Intelligence** | AI, networks, consciousness — human nodes, the Torus | Relay 12 (Programmable Humans / Torus) | #a855f7 |

**Data file:** `data/json/webTypes.json`

The progression Natural → Machine → Information → Intelligence mirrors the Odyssey's central arc: **from Calories to Consciousness**.

## USAGE ACROSS THE TRILOGY

**Volume 1 (Perspective / Story):** The 9 threads are woven into the narrative of each relay. Each chapter implicitly touches all 5 Webs within the context of its Web Type era.

**Volume 2 (Guide / Expansion):** The 9 threads become explicit analytical tools within the 4-Pillar Framework (Observational → Educational → Application → Thesis).

**Volume 3 (Game / Reality Engine):** The 9 threads are playable dimensions. The learner-player must identify which Webs and Web Types are active in each scenario and manage them strategically.

**The Manuals:** The Weave lives as a permanent reference framework — the structural DNA of the entire Odyssey.
