THE AD&D TRILOGY

Volume 3 follows the classic Advanced Dungeons & Dragons structure — three core books that together form the complete game system

Why AD&D? The original AD&D structure solved the same problem we face: how to teach a complex system through play. The Player's Handbook teaches the rules. The DM Guide teaches the teacher. The Monster Manual defines the challenges. Together, they create a complete learning ecosystem where the journey itself is the education.

THE IUMC METHODOLOGY

Every relay mission follows the 4-stage civil engineering methodology

I: IDENTIFY

Observational Stage. Recognise the relay's core infrastructure challenge. What was the problem humanity faced? What resources were available? What actually happened?

U: UNDERSTAND

Educational Stage. Analyse how different civilisations addressed the challenge. Discover inventions, biomimicry connections, and Counterparts comparisons. How to learn from it?

M: MANAGE & CONTROL

Application Stage. Apply the knowledge to solve problems. Complete challenge quizzes, engage with DAVID on deeper questions, and connect discoveries across relays.

C: COMPLETE (THESIS)

Thesis Stage. Demonstrate mastery through synthesis. Connect your relay's lessons to the broader framework — webs, threats, SDGs, and cross-relay patterns. What is your perspective?
