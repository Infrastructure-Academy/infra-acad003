Significance for iAAi

The BR explicitly includes “Publishing Services” alongside Engineering and Trading. This means 4ECL is already legally authorised to conduct book publishing, ebook sales, print-on-demand distribution, and all digital product sales in Hong Kong without requiring any additional licensing or registration amendments.

Combined with “Engineering” and “Trading,” the registration covers the full scope of iAAi’s commercial activities: consulting, platform operation, book sales, merchandise, IP licensing, and data intelligence services.

Hong Kong Tax Jurisdiction

Sales Tax / VAT
0%
HK charges zero sales tax, VAT, or GST on all goods and services including ebooks and digital products
Profits Tax (First HK$2M)
8.25%
Two-tier system — first HK$2M (~US$256K) of assessable profits at concessionary rate
Profits Tax (Above HK$2M)
16.5%
Standard rate for profits exceeding the HK$2M threshold
Withholding Tax
0%
No withholding tax on royalties, dividends, or payments to non-residents

Territorial Principle: HK taxes only profits sourced within Hong Kong. International online sales where the customer is overseas and the transaction is concluded via a website hosted outside HK may not be subject to HK profits tax. This is a significant structural advantage for a globally-distributed digital product.
