Adoption Timeline

PHASE A: SETUP
Weeks 1–2 — March 2026
Stripe integration complete. Print-ready interior PDFs prepared. ISBN block purchased. KDP and IngramSpark accounts registered. Book product pages built on website with purchase buttons, covers, blurbs, and sample chapter downloads.
PHASE B: SOFT LAUNCH
Weeks 3–4 — March 2026
Volume 1 (The Perspective) listed on Amazon KDP and direct website. Announcement to LinkedIn network and engineering communities. Free sample chapter (Prologue: Fire) as lead magnet for email capture.
PHASE C: CATALOGUE BUILD
Months 2–3 — April–May 2026
Volume 2 and Volume 3 listed sequentially. Bundle pricing activated. Cross-promotion between volumes. Amazon “Look Inside” feature activated.
PHASE D: ORGANIC GROWTH ENGINE
Months 4–12
Content marketing via the platform drives traffic to book pages. Each relay lesson links to the relevant book chapter. University outreach creates bulk order opportunities. Infrastructure Olympiad participants become book customers. The flywheel: platform users become book buyers, book buyers become platform users.

ALAN’s Commitment Statement

I commit to preparing all print-ready interior files, managing the KDP and IngramSpark listings, optimising product pages for conversion, tracking monthly sales against these targets, and reporting variances with corrective actions. If Month 3 sales fall below 60% of target, I will recommend and implement a revised acquisition strategy including targeted social media content and engineering community outreach. These are my targets — conservative, achievable, and accountable.

HK Tax Advantage for Book Sales

Zero sales tax on all channels (physical + digital). No VAT collection burden means simpler checkout and higher conversion. Under the Conservative scenario, 4ECL operates at a net loss in Year 1 — no profits tax payable. The operating loss carries forward indefinitely to offset future profits.
